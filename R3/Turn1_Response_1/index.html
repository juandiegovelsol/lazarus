Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Don't overmix!
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.

Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. On a large platter, alternately arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  2. Tuck the fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Grilled Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, and thyme.
  2. Place salmon fillets in a large resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the salmon, seal the bag, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove salmon from the marinade and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes per side, or until it flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges.
